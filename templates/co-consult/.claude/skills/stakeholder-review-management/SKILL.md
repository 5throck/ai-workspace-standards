## Overview

This skill provides a structured approach to managing stakeholder review and approval cycles, ensuring consulting deliverables receive appropriate input and sign-off before progression.

## When to Use This Skill

- Trigger: "Review cycle" or "Stakeholder sign-off" or "Feedback management"
- Use Case: Managing deliverable review gates during Phase 4 delivery
- Phase: Phase 4 (Implementation & Delivery)

## Steps

### Step 1: Plan Review Gates
- Identify which deliverables require formal review
- Map required approvers per deliverable
- Define review timeline and decision criteria

### Step 2: Distribute for Review
- Share deliverables with appropriate stakeholders
- Provide clear review instructions and feedback deadline
- Track who has and has not responded

### Step 3: Consolidate Feedback
- Aggregate feedback from all reviewers
- Identify conflicting inputs and resolve with engagement leadership
- Document accepted vs. deferred feedback

### Step 4: Obtain Sign-off
- Revise deliverable based on accepted feedback
- Obtain formal sign-off from required approvers
- Archive approved deliverables with version control

## Expected Outputs

- Review plan with approvers and timelines
- Consolidated feedback log
- Signed-off deliverable register
