# co-consult Variant Phases

## Domain Workflow

The co-consult variant follows a strategy consulting engagement workflow:

**Research → Analysis → Synthesis → Deliverable → Delivery**

## Phase Definitions

### Phase 0: Engagement Setup & Team Assembly
**Purpose**: Consulting engagement kick-off and team assembly
- Define engagement scope and client objectives
- Assign Engagement Leader, Strategy Analyst, Industry Expert
- Identify required skills (competitive-intelligence, stakeholder-alignment)

### Phase 1: Research & Discovery
**Purpose**: Gather and validate client context
- Industry Expert: Market landscape and context research
- Strategy Analyst: Data collection and stakeholder mapping
- Competitive Intelligence skill: Competitor benchmarking

### Phase 2: Analysis & Insight Development
**Purpose**: Generate insights from research
- Strategy Analyst: Quantitative and qualitative analysis
- Financial Modeling skill: Business case modeling (if required)
- Insight Synthesis skill: Pattern identification and insight framing

### Phase 3: Solution Design
**Purpose**: Develop recommendations and solutions
- Engagement Leader: Strategic recommendation framework
- Solutions Architect: Technical feasibility and implementation design
- Change Management Partner: Org readiness assessment
- **User approval gate**

### Phase 4: Deliverable Creation
**Purpose**: Produce client-ready outputs
- Communications Lead: Executive presentation and narrative
- Technical Writer: Detailed report and documentation
- Executive Presentation skill: Slide deck and talking points

### Phase 5: Review & Refinement
**Purpose**: Quality review and client alignment
- Stakeholder Review Management skill: Review cycle management
- Engagement Leader: Client feedback integration
- Max 2 iterations before escalation

### Phase 6: Delivery & Handoff
**Purpose**: Final delivery and project close
- memlog → sync
- Create PR with engagement deliverables
- Project delivery documentation

## Specialist Agents

| Phase | Agent | Responsibility |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| Research | industry-expert | Market and domain research |
| Analysis | strategy-analyst | Quantitative analysis and insight development |
| Solution | engagement-leader | Strategic recommendation framework |
| Solution | solutions-architect | Technical feasibility design |
| Change | change-management-partner | Org readiness and change impact |
| Delivery | communications-lead | Executive presentation and narrative |
